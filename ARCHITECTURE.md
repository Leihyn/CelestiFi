## Core Services (9 Components)

### 1. Whale Detector
- Monitors all Swap events
- Calculates USD values
- Triggers on threshold breach
- Stores whale history

### 2. Alert Engine
- 6 alert types
- Custom filtering
- Real-time notifications
- User preference management

### 3. Wallet Tracker
- Track specific addresses
- P&L calculations
- Trade history
- Leaderboard rankings

### 4. Pool Health Monitor
- Health scoring (0-100)
- Risk assessment
- Volatility tracking
- Liquidity analysis

### 5. Arbitrage Scanner
- Cross-pool price comparison
- Profit calculations
- Route optimization
- Opportunity tracking

### 6. MEV Detector
- Sandwich attack detection
- Front-running identification
- Attack pattern matching
- Profit/loss calculation

### 7. Price Impact Predictor
- Pre-trade impact calculation
- Slippage estimation
- Trade split suggestions
- AMM formula simulation

### 8. Impact Analyzer
- Market depth analysis
- Price simulation
- Trade recommendations

### 9. Socket Handler
- WebSocket management
- Real-time broadcasting
- Event distribution
- Connection pooling
